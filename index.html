We are Family Business manufacture of Mouth Blown Glass: Christmas Ornaments, Candle Holders, Candle Sticks, Vases, Birds Feeders and Glassware in Egypt. We work since 1997 and we operate our business through our Office Import & Export Cloud in USA. Our Factory is located in Alexandria – Egypt. We supply Big Chains and other smaller customers in USA , FRANCE, ITALY, GERMANY, GREECE, and JAPAN
We offer one of the world’s most extensive and best-loved collections of mouth-blown glass ornaments. All Christmas glass ornaments are made with traditional methods. Baubles are blown on a free fire from a glass pipe without any mold. Then the inner sides of the shape are manually plated with genuine Gold and finally hand painted and decorated. We use only high-quality of European Gold, ecological paints and lacquers. We pack our products only in Bubble paper however, Window Gift Box is available for (0.40 to 0.70 U.S.Cent) Shipping Price per item is 0.35 U.S Cent with minimum order 6000 item
